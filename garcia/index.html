Discover Mabini, Batangas

Diving, Hiking, Beaches & Local Delicacies

Featured Attractions

World-Class Diving Spots

Explore the underwater paradise of Mabini with its rich marine biodiversity, coral gardens, and crystal-clear waters perfect for both beginners and experienced divers.

Premium Dive Resorts

Stay at world-class dive resorts offering PADI certification courses, equipment rental, and guided underwater adventures in Anilao's pristine waters.

Mt. Gulugod Baboy Trek

Conquer the scenic trails of Mt. Gulugod Baboy and witness panoramic views of Batangas coastline, Taal Lake, and the surrounding mountain ranges.

Camp Netanya Resort & Spa

Experience Santorini-inspired luxury, infinity pools, and stunning sea views at Camp Netanya Resort & Spa in Mabini.

More Attractions

Anilao Diving Capital

Discover the underwater wonders of Anilao, known as the birthplace of scuba diving in the Philippines with over 40 dive sites.

Underwater Photography

Capture stunning underwater moments with macro photography of nudibranchs, coral formations, and marine life unique to Anilao waters.

Monte Carlo Resort

Experience luxury accommodations with stunning ocean views, infinity pools, and world-class amenities at Monte Carlo Resort in Mabini.

Batangas Lomi & Seafood

Taste authentic Batangas lomi, fresh seafood, and local delicacies at seaside restaurants and local eateries.

Sea's Spring Resort

Relax at Sea's Spring Resort featuring natural spring pools, beachfront accommodations, and serene coastal views perfect for a peaceful getaway.

Scuba Diving Courses

Learn to dive with certified PADI instructors offering Open Water, Advanced, and specialty courses in Anilao's calm, clear waters.

Ready to Explore Mabini?

Start planning your adventure to Mabini, Batangas today. From diving and hiking to beaches and local cuisine, create memories that will last a lifetime.
